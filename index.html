Welcome

Owned and operated by Kathryn Harra, Whispering Heart Equestrian seeks to connect horse and rider to create a lasting bond. With over 20 years of teaching and training experience, Kathryn enjoys helping horse and rider reach their fullest potential in a relaxed, encouraging environment.
